Welcome to
SKY ASYLUM

We are a New England-based indie film company. Since our start in 2002, we have worked in feature and documentary formats, as well as special events and weddings.

In addition to our own productions, we have worked in association with Purple Crayon Productions for the sci-fi The Present (accepted at festivals in New York and New Hampshire), and with Fox C. Flix for the crime thriller, Dominoes.
